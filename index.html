Akan Name Generator

This webpage is designed to take the input of your gender and date of birth and calculate the day you were born. Based off of this information the webpage will also return to you what your Akan Name is.

Akan names are derived from Ghanian culture. Frequently in Ghana, children are given their first name as a 'day name' which corresponds to the day in the week they were born. Here are Ghanian day names.

Male

    Sunday: Kwasi
    Monday: Kwadwo
    Tuesday: Kwabena
    Wednesday: Kwaku
    Thursday: Yaw
    Friday: Kofi
    Saturday: Kwame

Female

    Sunday: Akosua
    Monday: Adwoa
    Tuesday: Abenaa
    Wednesday: Akua
    Thursday: Yaa
    Friday: Afua
    Saturday: Ama
